# Data and Rights Note

This repository contains:

- a curated text-only Kangaroo mathematics corpus,
- derived teacher annotations,
- condition-specific SFT JSONLs,
- evaluation outputs,
- analysis tables,
- and thesis source.

## Intellectual Property and Distribution

The underlying Math Kangaroo competition materials remain the intellectual
property of Math Kangaroo Switzerland (Mathe K\"anguru Schweiz).

Public distribution of this repository is permitted for scientific and academic
purposes. The materials in this repository should not be used for personal
profit or other commercial exploitation.

This applies in particular to:

- the curated problem texts,
- the official-solution-derived examples,
- the derived JSONL training and evaluation files,
- and the associated artifact-backed reproducibility materials.

## Intended Use

This repository is intended for:

- scientific reproducibility,
- academic supervision and review,
- research archiving,
- and non-commercial scholarly reuse.

Users of the repository should preserve attribution to Math Kangaroo
Switzerland as the intellectual property owner of the original competition
materials.
